Theresa's room, for example, is a small one located directly underneath the taproom/pubroom. Chrys is using it to leave now.</p><p>To continue my cellphone story from Monday, it turns out there's nothing I can do to get this phone on my network. It just isn't happening, which is a shame. Not that I use the data much on my antique smartphone, sadly.</p><p>See everyone Monday!</p>
