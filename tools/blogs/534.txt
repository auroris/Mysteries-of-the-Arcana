Without them, this comic would just be a dead spot on the internet that never updated.</p><p>12 people.</p><p>I know more than 12 people read Mysteries of the Arcana, either page by page or in chunks every few weeks. Our metrics say so. So, I'm gonna ask again.</p><p><a href="https://www.patreon.com/user?u=452395" target="_blank">Please consider donating to our Patreon</a>, even if it is only a dollar or two a month. Every single dollar counts. Right now, we're $69/month away from being able to return to two updates per week. Keith and I would love to get back to a faster story progression.</p><p>So, think about it, please. Is Mysteries of the Arcana worth supporting?</p><p>Thank you.</p><p>New Bricks of the Arcana on Friday. New story comic next Wednesday.</p>
