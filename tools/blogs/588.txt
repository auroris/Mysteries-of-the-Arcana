   <div id="avatar"><img src="http://mysteriesofthearcana.com/images/spacer.gif" border="0" alt="JGray"><br><span class="desc"><center>JGray </center></span></div>    <p>And a happy Thursday morning to everyone. The end credits will make you so very, very happy.</p><p>See you Monday!</p>
