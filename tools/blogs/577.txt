   <div id="avatar"><img src="http://mysteriesofthearcana.com/images/spacer.gif" border="0" alt="JGray"><br><span class="desc"><center>JGray </center></span></div>    <p>That Theresa. She can't see a gun without wanting to get her hands on it...</p><p>More seriously, when I was writing this chapter Keith and I had a lot of back and forth discussion on what sort of firearms the viquirrels would be carrying. I'm glad his knowledge of firearms is so much more comprehensive than mine.</p><p>Has anyone read Jim Butcher's new book yet? I've got it on hold at the library but I'm 18th in line for it. I don't expect to read it until sometime in November.</p>
