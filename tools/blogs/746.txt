When I was growing up, my dad's best friend was a gunsmith, and we had an interesting day test firing guns (I wasn't allowed to touch the uzi, but he did demonstrate it). The issue is less about ability, and more about accuracy. The gun kicks such that after the first shot in full auto, you won't be able to hit anything.</p><div><br></div><div>The pose and idea was more for "cool factor" than actually being practical.</div><p></p>
